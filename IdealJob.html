I don't really have an ideal job in mind but the closest thing to one is becoming a clinical/organizational psychologist.

I will also need to find work during my studies relevant to the field I want to pursue to gain the necessary experience for the job.
The experience I currently have is very minimal as I am only in semester two of my first year with a lot to learn. In high school I did psychology for two years which has also added to my general knowledge regarding psychology.
