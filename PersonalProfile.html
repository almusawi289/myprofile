Test Results

The results of these tests are relatively close to what I had expected, depicting me as a person who is 'in between' when it comes to a lot of factors.
In the learning style test, I am shown to be an auditory learner- acquiring much of my information through a combination of visual and auditory means. For the most part I believe this is relatively accurate.

In the Myers Briggs test I was attributed the "virtuoso" personality type. The personality is said to be an adventurous type of personality that is curious and make up only 5% of the population.
Virtuosos are said to explore ideas through creating, trouble shooting, trial and error and first-hand experience.
I identify to many of these characteristics but not all of them as I believe a personality is too intricate and complex to be identified through mere personality tests.

In the Big Five factors test I scored highly on extroversion and agreeableness. Yet low scores on intellect/imagination and conscientiousness.
The results contradict those from the Myers Briggs test further indicating the lack of reliability of these tests- though there is an element of truth to them. This could also just be a case of the Barnum effect however.
According to these tests I would functional well in a group so long as the members aren't too quiet and aggressively assertive. Having scored high on agreeableness I would supposedly know how to deal with people well due to being able to empathize ad show kindness.
